MOHD ASHRAF MIR

MOHD ASHRAF MIR.., Thousands gather to pay tribute to Army braveheart Ashraf Mir, yells anti-Pakistan slogans Thousands of Kashmiris turned up to bid farewell to Indian braveheart Subedar Mohd Ashraf Mir, who was killed in the Sunjuwan attack by Jaish-e-Mohammad terrorists. Localites even reportedly yelled anti-Pakistan slogans at the funeral.
